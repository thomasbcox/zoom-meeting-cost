Privacy Policy

Effective date: 1 June 2026 · Meeting Cost Meter

Meeting Cost Meter is built to be privacy-preserving by design. The rate information you enter stays in your own browser, and the app's server never receives it.

The short version

Rate data stays on your device. The hourly rates, default rate, and loaded-cost multiplier you enter are stored only in your own browser's localStorage. They are never sent to our server.

No participant data is stored. The app does not store participant information on the server or keep it after the meeting ends.

The server only sees aggregate numbers. It receives sanitized, aggregate cost figures only — never individual rates or names.

No HR or identity systems. The app does not connect to HR, payroll, SSO, or any employee directory.

What the app is

Meeting Cost Meter is a Zoom App that displays the live estimated cost of a meeting. A presenter enters a private, best-guess table of hourly rates, a default rate, and a loaded-cost multiplier. The app multiplies those estimates by the elapsed time and the number of participants to produce a running cost, which can be shown as an overlay on the presenter's camera feed.

Rate data you enter

The rate table, default rate, and loaded-cost multiplier are entered by the presenter and are stored only in that presenter's browser, using the browser's localStorage. This data:

  • never leaves the presenter's device as individual rate data;
  • is not transmitted to, or stored on, our server;
  • is not shared with other meeting participants; and
  • can be cleared at any time by clearing it in the app or clearing your browser's site data.

Because rate data lives in your browser, it is specific to that browser and device. Using a different browser or device, or clearing site data, starts fresh.

What the server receives

To render the on-camera overlay, the app sends the server a small, sanitized snapshot of aggregate meeting state — for example the running total cost, the cost per minute, the elapsed time, and the participant count. This snapshot:

  • contains no individual rates and no participant names or identities;
  • reflects only combined, summary figures; and
  • is used to display the live cost during the active meeting.

No participant data is persisted on the server beyond the active meeting session. The app does not build profiles, does not track individuals across meetings, and does not retain meeting cost history on the server.

What the app does not do

  • It does not integrate with HR, payroll, SSO, or any employee directory.
  • It does not read real salary or compensation data — all rates are the presenter's own estimates.
  • It does not record audio, video, chat, or meeting content.
  • It does not sell data or use it for advertising.

Information from Zoom

To run inside a meeting, the app uses the Zoom Apps SDK to obtain the in-meeting context it needs (such as the current participant count) and, where required, an authorization token. This information is used in real time to compute and display the live cost and is not stored on our server beyond the active session.

Data retention

Rate data persists only in your browser until you clear it. Aggregate cost state exists only during the active meeting and is not retained on the server afterward.

Removing the app

You can remove Meeting Cost Meter at any time from the Zoom App Marketplace. To also remove the rate data held in your browser, clear the app's data or your browser's site data for this app.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, we will update the effective date above and post the revised policy at this address.
