Support

Need help with Meeting Cost Meter? We're glad to help.

Email support: thomas+mcsupport@txl-lab.com

We aim to respond within two business days.

When you write, please include

  • a short description of what you were trying to do and what happened;
  • the browser and operating system you were using;
  • whether you were in a Zoom meeting on desktop or mobile; and
  • a screenshot, if the issue is something you can see on screen.

Please do not include anyone's real salary or compensation data — the app never needs it, and we don't want it.

Common questions

Where are my rates stored?

In your own browser, using localStorage. They never reach our server. See the Privacy Policy for details.

My rates disappeared. Why?

Because rate data lives in your browser, it is tied to that specific browser and device. Switching browsers or devices, using private/incognito mode, or clearing your browser's site data will reset your rates.

Do participants see my individual rates?

No. Participants only ever see the combined, estimated cost on the overlay. Your per-person rate table stays private to your browser.

Are the costs accurate?

They are estimates based on the rates you enter, not real compensation data. See the Terms of Use.

How do I remove the app?

Uninstall it from the Zoom App Marketplace. To also clear the rate data in your browser, clear the app's data or your browser's site data for this app.
