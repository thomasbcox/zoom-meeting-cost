Support

Effective date: 9 July 2026 · Meeting Cost Meter

Need help with Meeting Cost Meter? Here's how to reach us.

Email support: thomas+mcsupport@txl-lab.com

We usually reply within two business days.

When you write, please include

  • a short description of what you were trying to do and what happened;
  • the browser and operating system you were using;
  • whether you were in a Zoom meeting on desktop or mobile; and
  • a screenshot, if the issue is something you can see on screen.

The app asks for opportunity cost, not pay. Please do not include anyone's real salary or compensation data — the app never needs it, and we don't want it.

Common questions

What number do I enter?

You enter one average hourly opportunity cost and the number of attendees. It's opportunity cost — the value of the best work someone could be doing instead of being in the meeting — not their wage or salary. See the Documentation for why.

Where are my settings stored?

They aren't stored — your settings are session-only. The figures you enter stay in the app for the current meeting and are cleared when it ends; nothing is saved on our server or tied to your Zoom account, and no meeting participant or other user can see them. See the Privacy Policy for details.

My settings didn't save. Why?

They're session-only by design — Meeting Cost Meter doesn't save your settings between meetings. The figures you enter live in the app for the current meeting and reset when it ends, so you set them fresh each time.

The cost overlay isn't appearing on my camera

First, make sure your camera is on and that you've enabled the overlay in the app's side panel. Then confirm you're using the Zoom Workplace desktop app, version 7.1.0 or later — on older desktop clients the on-camera overlay may not render, even when everything is set up correctly. See the Documentation for details.

Do participants see the rate I entered?

No. Participants only ever see the combined, estimated cost on the overlay — the hourly rate you enter is never shown to them.

Are the costs accurate?

They are estimates based on the opportunity-cost values you enter, not real compensation data. See the Terms of Use.

How do I remove the app and my data?

Uninstall it from the Zoom App Marketplace. There's no saved configuration to delete — your settings are session-only and are already cleared when the meeting ends. Questions? Email thomas+mcsupport@txl-lab.com.
