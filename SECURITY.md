# Security Policy

Meeting Cost Meter is a Zoom App by Transformative Leadership Lab LLC.

## Reporting a vulnerability

Please report security issues **privately** by email to **thomas+mcsupport@txl-lab.com**
with the subject line `SECURITY`. Do **not** open a public GitHub issue for security
reports.

When reporting, please include:

- a description of the issue and its potential impact,
- steps to reproduce (proof-of-concept if possible),
- the affected URL/endpoint or component, and
- any relevant logs or screenshots (no real credentials, please).

We aim to acknowledge reports within **2 business days** and to coordinate a fix and
disclosure timeline with you. Please give us a reasonable opportunity to remediate before
any public disclosure.

## Scope

- This repository (the Meeting Cost Meter client and server) and its hosted service.

## Out of scope

- Findings that require a compromised device or Zoom account.
- Volumetric denial-of-service testing.
- Social engineering of the maintainers.

## How we handle reports

Reports are triaged and remediated under our vulnerability-management and incident-response
processes. See the policy set in [`dev-docs/policies/`](dev-docs/policies/) and the public
[Security overview](https://thomasbcox.github.io/zoom-meeting-cost/security.html) and
[Privacy Policy](https://thomasbcox.github.io/zoom-meeting-cost/privacy.html).
